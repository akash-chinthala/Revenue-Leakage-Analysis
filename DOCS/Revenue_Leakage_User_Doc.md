# Revenue Leakage Detection & Analysis

## 🧠 Business Context
Revenue leakage occurs due to missing payments or process inefficiencies.

## 🎯 Objective
- Detect financial discrepancies
- Improve payment tracking

## 🔍 Approach
Compared expected vs actual revenue.

## 💡 Key Insights
- Missing payments directly impact revenue
- Some cancelled orders still have payments

## 🚀 Business Impact
- Improved financial accuracy
- Reduced revenue loss
